About Me

Hello! I'm Sophie, a digital artist and web developer with a passion for creating whimsical, Ghibli-inspired designs. My work blends technical skill with artistic vision to craft experiences that feel magical and alive.

When I'm not coding or drawing, you can find me hiking in the woods, tending to my garden, or curled up with a sketchbook and a cup of tea.

I believe in the power of technology to tell stories and create connections, and I strive to bring warmth and personality to every project I work on.
