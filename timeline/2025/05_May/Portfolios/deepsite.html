About Me

Like the characters in a Miyazaki film, I believe in the magic of imagination and the power of storytelling. My journey began when I first watched "Spirited Away" and realized that digital experiences could transport people to other worlds.

With 5 years of experience in web development and design, I specialize in creating interfaces that feel alive, whimsical, and full of personality - just like your favorite Ghibli characters.

When I'm not coding, you can find me painting watercolor landscapes, tending to my plant collection, or rewatching "Howl's Moving Castle" for the hundredth time.
